Class: Track

Track(source, optsopt)

A Track represents a single audio asset in its entirety. It is an asset intended for production purposes. This class offers interfaces to ffmpeg, sox, fpcalc, and audiowaveform to produce industry-standard analyses and generated assets, such as spectral imagery and audio fingerprints.
